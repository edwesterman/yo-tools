International Coastal Cleanup elizabeth.karron@yale.edu Liz Karron 09-19

On September 19, designated as International Coastal Cleanup Day by the Ocean Conservancy, people around the world will work to clean up the trash found on their local beaches and waterways. These volunteers have a big impact: last year, 560,000 volunteers in 91 countries picked up more than 16 million pounds of trash. Become part of the effort this year by participating in the coastal cleanup event at the Long Wharf Nature Preserve in New Haven at 10am!
