The Thimble Islands, CT

THIMBLE ISLANDS 'YAKING (kayaking)! http://goo.gl/forms/EabaaQzgbf william.liang@yale.edu Will Liang and jonathan.adler@yale.edu Jonathan Adler April 18th ~$60

YO is heading out to the CT coast! Join Jonathan and Will for a kayaking trip in the Branford Thimble Islands on Saturday, April 18th--we'll check out "Money Island", "East and West Crib" and Yale's own Horse Island. We'll depart from Phelps gate at 9:30 am and plan to return by 3pm. Gear will be about $60/person (depending on interest). Preference will be given to those who can provide cars!

*Our route may see some high winds and fast currents, so we're limiting this trip to participants with kayaking experience.

Please let us know by this Friday if you want to come!

AT Overnight http://goo.gl/forms/bM8QQkPC6w angus.mossman@yale.edu Angus Mossman and eli.westerman@yale.edu Eli Westerman 04-25 thru 04-26 $30

Start your reading period off on the right foot with a hike on the Appalachian Trail/Housatonic State Forest in northwestern CT. We will leave from Yale early on Saturday, April 25 and hike approximately 12 miles over two days returning to campus late Sunday afternoon. Participants should have previous backpacking experience, but this will not be an extremely strenuous hike, and there will be time to poke around side trails and waterfalls. Preference given to those with cars.
