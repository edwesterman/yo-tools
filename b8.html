Woohoo! It's (almost) Spring Break! The weather finally looks promising this Friday morning, so start your spring break off with a short jaunt up West Rock park. We'll leave from Phelps Gate at 05:35 and we'll be back by 7:30. Preference given to those with cars and those who have signed up before but been unable to go (please indicate this). IT WILL BE COLD... so be sure to layer up. Also, the road is unplowed and covered in snow, so good footwear is essential.

SpringYO: Castle Craig Day Hike http://goo.gl/forms/3ytuRXlHRq claire.chang@yale.edu Claire Chang , aaron.troncoso@yale.edu Aaron Troncoso and rachel.strodel@yale.edu Rachel Strodel 03-22 $3

Want to spend the last day of spring vacation hiking to a hundred-year old tower with an observation deck? Join YO leaders for a day hike to Hubbard Park in Meriden, CT. We will leave campus at 9:30 and be back by 4:30 pm. The hike will be beautiful, and a highlight of the trip will be a delicious picnic at the top of Castle Craig with views of the foothills of the Berkshires and the summit of Mount Tom.

SpringYO: Wadsworth Falls Day Hike http://goo.gl/forms/PeYRQQMEm3 catherine.harmer@yale.edu Katie Harmer and sofia.carrera@yale.edu Sofia Carrera 03-20 $5

Come hiking at beautiful Wadsworth Falls on Friday March 20th. We'll leave campus at 10am and be back by 3pm. We'll hike on a 3.5 mile (round trip) path along a river to a 20ft waterfall where we'll enjoy a wonderful picnic! We'll have time to explore some side trails, too. No hiking experience necessary!

SpringYO: Bike Series http://goo.gl/forms/vwg5R53DQH zhengxiang.toh@yale.edu Toh Zheng Xiang Spring Break Week 2

Come explore different parks and towns close by New Haven! We are doing a series of bike trips to different spots close by:

  1. Lighthouse Point (~12 miles)
  2. Wentworth's Ice Cream/ Sleeping Giant State Park (~20 miles)
  3. Silver Sands State Park (~28 miles)
  4. Hammonasset Beach State Park (~50 miles)
  5. 2-day trip along the Farmington Canal Trail to Massachusetts (~140 miles)

Meant for beginners, no experience needed for the one-day trips (but please have a helmet and don't come on a zagster!). Watch for more details regarding dates and price next week!
